# Seed Level - Lesson 10: Shopping & Money
## Slide-by-Slide Lesson Plan

---

**Level:** Seed (A1)  
**Lesson:** 10 - Shopping & Money

## SLIDE 1: TITLE SLIDE
### Shopping & Money

**Visual:** Store icons, shopping bags, coins, dollar signs, and price tags

**Core focus:**
- Shopping vocabulary
- Money and price words
- "How much is it?" / "It's ___ dollars."
- "Can I have...?" / "I want..."

## SLIDE 4: ASKING ABOUT PRICE
### "How much is it?"

**Question pattern:**
```text
How much is it?
```

**Answers:**
- It's five dollars.
- It's ten dollars.
- It's twenty dollars.

**More examples:**
```text
How much is the shirt?
It's twelve dollars.

How much is this bag?
It's eight dollars.
```

**Important note:**
- Use **How much** for price
- Use **It is / It's** in the answer

---

## SLIDE 5: ASKING FOR THINGS
### "Can I have...?" and "I want..."

| Structure | Use | Example |
|-----------|-----|---------|
| **Can I have + item?** | polite request | Can I have this book? |
| **I want + item.** | simple direct statement | I want a jacket. |
| **I want + this/that + item.** | choosing something | I want that hat. |

**Model sentences:**
- Can I have this shirt, please?
- I want these shoes.
- Can I have the blue bag?
- I want a cheap jacket.

**Polite word:**
- **please**

## SLIDE 7: DESCRIBING PRICES
### Cheap or Expensive?

**Main adjectives:**
- **cheap**
- **expensive**

**Pattern:**
```text
It is + cheap / expensive.
```

**Examples:**
- This T-shirt is cheap.
- That phone is expensive.
- The book is not expensive.
- These shoes are very cheap.

**Compare simple ideas:**
- "This bag is cheap."
- "That bag is expensive."

---

## SLIDE 8: SIMPLE SHOPPING DIALOGUE
### At the Store

**Model dialogue:**

```text
Customer: How much is this shirt?
Store Clerk: It's fifteen dollars.
Customer: Can I have this shirt, please?
Store Clerk: Yes, of course.
Customer: Thank you.
```

**Another model:**

```text
Customer: How much is that bag?
Store Clerk: It's twenty dollars.
Customer: I want that bag.
```

**Useful expressions:**
- How much is this...?
- It's ___ dollars.
- Can I have...?
- I want...

---

## SLIDE 9: COMBINING IDEAS
### Item + Color + Price

**Pattern 1:**
```text
This/That + item + is + price.
```

**Pattern 2:**
```text
The + color + item + is + price.
```

**Examples:**
- This hat is ten dollars.
- That dress is twenty-five dollars.
- The blue shirt is fifteen dollars.
- The black shoes are thirty dollars.

**Adding opinion:**
- The red bag is cheap.
- The jacket is expensive, but I like it.

---

## SLIDE 10: COMMON MISTAKES
### Watch Out

| ❌ Incorrect | ✅ Correct |
|-------------|------------|
| How much this is? | How much is this? |
| It five dollars. | It is five dollars. |
| I want this shoes. | I want these shoes. |
| Can I have a this bag? | Can I have this bag? |
| This shirt are cheap. | This shirt is cheap. |
| The shoes is expensive. | The shoes are expensive. |
| It is ten dollar. | It is ten dollars. |

**Final model:**
```text
How much is this jacket?
It's twenty dollars.
Can I have it, please?
```
