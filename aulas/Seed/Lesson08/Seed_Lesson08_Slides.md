# Seed Level - Lesson 08: Weather & Seasons
## Slide-by-Slide Lesson Plan

---

## 🎯 LESSON INFO
**Level:** Seed (A1)  
**Lesson:** 08 - Weather & Seasons  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Group Size:** 1-5 students

---

## SLIDE 1: TITLE SLIDE
### Weather & Seasons

**Visual:** Bright weather icons and the four seasons around the title

**Core focus:**
- Weather vocabulary
- Seasons of the year
- "What's the weather like?"
- "It's sunny / rainy / hot / cold"

---

## SLIDE 4: MAIN QUESTION
### What's the weather like?

**Question pattern:**
```text
What's the weather like?
```

**Answers:**
- It's sunny.
- It's cloudy.
- It's hot and windy.
- It's cold today.

**Useful variation:**
```text
What's the weather like in winter?
It's cold in winter.
```

---

## SLIDE 5: STRUCTURE WITH "IT'S"
### How to Describe the Weather

**Main structure:**
```text
It's + weather adjective.
```

**Examples:**
- It's sunny.
- It's rainy.
- It's warm.
- It's cool.

**Important note:**
- ✅ It is sunny.
- ❌ Is sunny.

---

## SLIDE 6: TEMPERATURE WORDS
### Hot, Warm, Cool, Cold

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **hot** | muito quente | It's hot in summer. |
| **warm** | quente agradável | It's warm in spring. |
| **cool** | fresco | It's cool at night. |
| **cold** | frio | It's cold in winter. |

**Temperature line:**
```text
HOT → WARM → COOL → COLD
```

---

## SLIDE 7: COMBINING IDEAS
### Weather + Season

**Pattern 1:**
```text
In + season, it's + adjective.
```

**Pattern 2:**
```text
It's + adjective + and + adjective.
```

**Examples:**
- In summer, it's hot and sunny.
- In winter, it's cold and rainy.
- In fall, it's cool and windy.
- In spring, it's warm and cloudy.

---

## SLIDE 8: COMMON MISTAKES
### Watch Out

| ❌ Incorrect | ✅ Correct |
|-------------|------------|
| Is sunny today. | It is sunny today. |
| The weather is sun. | The weather is sunny. |
| In summer is hot. | In summer, it is hot. |
| It's a cold. | It's cold. |
| What the weather like? | What's the weather like? |
| My favorite station is winter. | My favorite season is winter. |

**Final model:**
- What's the weather like today?
- It's warm and sunny.
