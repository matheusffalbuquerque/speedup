## 🔧 ESTRUTURAS LINGUÍSTICAS

### 1. Describing Weather with "It's..."

Usamos **It's + adjective** para falar do clima agora.

**Structure:**
```text
It's + weather adjective.
```

**Examples:**
- It's sunny.
- It's rainy.
- It's hot.
- It's cold.
- It's windy today.
- It's cloudy this morning.

**Important Note:**
- Em inglês, usamos **it** para falar do clima.
- Não dizemos: ~~Is sunny~~
- Dizemos: **It is sunny.**

**More examples in context:**
- It's hot in the afternoon.
- It's cold at night.
- It's rainy in my city today.
- It's warm and sunny in spring.

---

### 2. Asking About the Weather

A pergunta principal é:

**What's the weather like?**

**Possible Answers:**
- It's sunny.
- It's rainy.
- It's cloudy.
- It's hot and windy.
- It's cold today.

**Question and Answer Pattern:**

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| What's the weather like? | It's sunny. |
| What's the weather like today? | It's rainy today. |
| What's the weather like in winter? | It's cold in winter. |
| What's the weather like in your city? | It's hot and sunny. |

**Useful variation:**
- **How's the weather?**
  - It's good.
  - It's terrible.
  - It's very hot.

> 💡 **Teaching Tip:** "What's the weather like?" pergunta sobre a condição do clima, não sobre gosto pessoal.

---

### 3. Seasons of the Year

As estações podem aparecer com a preposição **in**.

**Structure:**
```text
in + season
```

**Examples:**
- In summer, it's hot.
- In winter, it's cold.
- In spring, it's warm and rainy.
- In fall, it's cool and windy.

**Quick Reference Table:**

| Season | Common Weather | Example Sentence |
|--------|----------------|------------------|
| **spring** | warm, rainy | In spring, the weather is warm. |
| **summer** | hot, sunny | In summer, it's very hot. |
| **fall / autumn** | cool, windy | In fall, it's cool and windy. |
| **winter** | cold, rainy / snowy | In winter, it's cold. |

**Note:**
- **Fall** é mais comum no inglês americano.
- **Autumn** é mais comum no inglês britânico.

---

### 4. Hot, Warm, Cool, Cold

Essas palavras ajudam a falar da temperatura com mais precisão.

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **hot** | muito quente | It's hot in summer. |
| **warm** | quente agradável | It's warm this morning. |
| **cool** | fresco | It's cool in the evening. |
| **cold** | frio | It's cold in winter. |

**Temperature Scale Idea:**

```text
HOT  →  WARM  →  COOL  →  COLD
```

**Examples:**
- It's hot in January.
- It's warm in the spring.
- It's cool at night.
- It's cold in the winter.

---

### 5. Combining Weather Words

Podemos combinar dois adjetivos para descrever melhor o clima.

**Pattern:**
```text
It's + adjective + and + adjective.
```

**Examples:**
- It's hot and sunny.
- It's cold and windy.
- It's cool and cloudy.
- It's warm and rainy.

**More examples with seasons:**
- In summer, it's hot and sunny.
- In winter, it's cold and rainy.
- In spring, it's warm and cloudy.
- In fall, it's cool and windy.

---

### 6. Common Mistakes

| Incorrect | Correct | Why |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| Is sunny today. | It is sunny today. | Weather needs **it** |
| The weather is sun. | The weather is sunny. | Use adjective, not noun |
| In summer is hot. | In summer, it is hot. | Sentence needs **it** |
| It's a cold. | It's cold. | No article before adjective |
| What the weather like? | What's the weather like? | Missing **is** |
| My favorite station is winter. | My favorite season is winter. | "Season", not "station" |

## 📖 3. READING ACTIVITY

### The Weather in Four Seasons

My name is Lucas, and I live in a city with different seasons. In **summer**, it's usually **hot and sunny**. I like going to the park and drinking cold juice. In **winter**, it's often **cold and rainy**, and I stay home more. In **spring**, the weather is **warm**, and I can see many flowers. In **fall**, it's usually **cool and windy**. I like fall because the weather is calm and comfortable.

My favorite season is spring because it is not too hot and not too cold.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. What is the weather like in summer?
2. What is the weather like in winter?
3. Why does Lucas like spring?
4. Which season is cool and windy?
5. What is your favorite season?

## 🎲 6. CLASS ACTIVITY

### Weather Charades

**How it works:**
1. One student picks a weather card.
2. The student acts the weather with gestures.
3. The other students guess:
   - It's rainy!
   - It's windy!
   - It's cold!

**Optional extension:**
- Students also say a season:
  - It's cold. It's winter.
