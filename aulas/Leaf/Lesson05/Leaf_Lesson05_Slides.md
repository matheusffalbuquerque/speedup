## Slide 2: Key Vocabulary 📚
### Core Words for Opinions

| Word / Expression | Translation | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **opinion** | opinião | *In my opinion, this app is very useful.* |
| **believe** | acreditar | *I believe that daily practice matters.* |
| **think** | achar, pensar | *I think the first option is better.* |
| **agree** | concordar | *I agree with your idea.* |
| **disagree** | discordar | *I disagree because it is too expensive.* |

| **argument** | argumento | *That is a strong argument.* |
| **perspective** | perspectiva | *From a teacher's perspective, rules are necessary.* |

## Slide 3: Opinion Phrases 💬
### How to Introduce What You Think

| Expression | Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **In my opinion, ...** | opinião direta | *In my opinion, school should start later.* |
| **I think (that) ...** | opinião informal | *I think this solution is practical.* |
| **I believe (that) ...** | opinião mais forte | *I believe that teamwork is essential.* |
| **From my perspective, ...** | ponto de vista analítico | *From my perspective, online learning saves time.* |
| **Personally, ...** | opinião pessoal | *Personally, I prefer studying at night.* |
| **As far as I'm concerned, ...** | opinião mais formal | *As far as I'm concerned, the rule is fair.* |

**Tip:** Avoid stacking openers.  
✅ *In my opinion, this is useful.*  
❌ *In my opinion, I think this is useful.*

## Slide 4: Agreeing ✅
### Useful Expressions for Agreement

| Expression | Meaning / Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **I agree.** | concordância direta | *I agree. That's the best option.* |
| **I agree with you.** | concorda com a pessoa | *I agree with you about that point.* |
| **You're right.** | validação | *You're right. We need more time.* |
| **That's true.** | confirmação | *That's true. It happens a lot.* |
| **Exactly.** | ênfase | *Exactly. That's the real problem.* |
| **Absolutely.** | concordância forte | *Absolutely. Communication is key.* |
| **I completely agree.** | concordância total | *I completely agree with that idea.* |
| **That's a fair point.** | reconhece valor no argumento | *That's a fair point.* |

**Partial agreement:**
- *I agree to some extent, but...*
- *I partly agree with you.*

## Slide 5: Disagreeing Politely ⚖️
### Stronger Idea, Softer Language

| Expression | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **I don't think so.** | discordância leve | *I don't think so. It seems unrealistic.* |
| **I don't agree.** | discordância direta | *I don't agree. The plan is too risky.* |
| **I'm not sure I agree.** | discordância educada | *I'm not sure I agree with that conclusion.* |
| **I see it differently.** | diplomático | *I see it differently. We need more data.* |
| **That may be true, but...** | contraponto equilibrado | *That may be true, but the cost is high.* |
| **On the other hand, ...** | contraste | *On the other hand, this option is faster.* |
| **I disagree because...** | com justificativa | *I disagree because it ignores the main issue.* |

**Natural pattern:**  
*I see your point, but I think...*

## Slide 6: Giving Reasons 🧠
### Opinion + Reason + Example

| Structure | Example |
|---|---|
| **I think... because...** | *I think public parks are important because they improve quality of life.* |
| **In my opinion... since...** | *In my opinion, online classes work well since they are flexible.* |
| **The main reason is that...** | *The main reason is that students need more speaking time.* |
| **For example, ...** | *For example, many workers save hours by not commuting.* |
| **This is why...** | *This is why I support shorter meetings.* |

**Model sentence:**  
*From my perspective, remote work is useful because it saves time. For example, people can use that time to study, rest, or exercise.*

## Slide 7: Common Mistakes 🚫
### Errors Brazilian Students Often Make

| ❌ Incorrect | ✅ Correct | Why? |
|---|---|---|
| *I am agree with you.* | *I agree with you.* | "Agree" is a verb |
| *I don't agree you.* | *I don't agree with you.* | Use **with** + person |
| *From my opinion...* | *In my opinion...* | Correct fixed expression |
| *I disagree with because...* | *I disagree because...* | No **with** before clause |
| *In my opinion I think...* | *In my opinion...* / *I think...* | Avoid repetition |
| *I think is better.* | *I think it is better.* | Clause needs a subject |

## Slide 8: Communication Strategy 🌱
### How to Sound Fluent and Respectful

**A simple 4-step formula:**

1. **State your opinion**  
   *In my opinion, ...*

2. **Show agreement or disagreement**  
   *I agree with you.* / *I'm not sure I agree.*

3. **Add a reason**  
   *because... / since... / the main reason is that...*

4. **Balance the idea when necessary**  
   *On the other hand, ... / That may be true, but...*

**Example:**

*In my opinion, online learning is very useful. I agree that face-to-face classes are important, but online lessons offer flexibility. The main reason is that students can study from anywhere.*
