# Root - Lesson 10: Environment & Nature
**Nível:** A1 Avançado / A2 Iniciante  
**Duração:** 60 minutos  
**Tema:** Meio ambiente e natureza

---

## 🎯 Lesson Focus
- Core vocabulary about nature, animals, plants, and environmental problems
- Modal **should** for advice and recommendations
- Environmental expressions with **It's important to...**
- Present Simple for facts and general truths
- Natural examples about protecting the environment

## Slide 3: Present Simple for General Truths

### Talking About Facts

Use the **Present Simple** for things that are generally true.

| Structure | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Nature/Animals/Plants + base idea** | "Nature gives us clean air." |
| **Subject + verb(s)** | "Trees help the environment." |
| **Subject + do/does not + base verb** | "Pollution does not help cities." |

### Model Sentences

- "Trees clean the air."
- "Rivers carry water to many places."
- "Animals need clean habitats."
- "Plants grow with water and light."
- "Pollution harms the environment."

---

## Slide 4: Using "Should" for Advice

### Giving Recommendations

Use **should** to say what is a good idea.

```text
Subject + should + base verb
```

### Examples

- "We should recycle more."
- "People should protect forests."
- "You should save water."
- "Cities should reduce pollution."
- "We should not throw trash in rivers."

### Quick Note

- **should** + base verb
- not: **should not / shouldn't**

---

## Slide 5: Useful Environmental Expressions

### Important Classroom Language

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| **It's important to...** | "It's important to save water." |
| **We need to...** | "We need to protect animals." |
| **We should...** | "We should plant more trees." |
| **We shouldn't...** | "We shouldn't waste electricity." |

### More Natural Examples

- "It's important to recycle at home."
- "We need to keep rivers clean."
- "We should use less plastic."
- "We shouldn't burn trash."

## Slide 6: Reading Model — Caring for Nature

### Short Text

> Sofia loves nature and wants to help the environment. She says people should recycle paper, plastic, and glass. She also believes it is important to save water and keep rivers clean. In her neighborhood, there are many trees and plants, and she thinks cities should protect these green areas. Sofia teaches her family that small actions can help animals, plants, and the planet.

### Language Highlights

- **loves nature**
- **should recycle**
- **it is important to save water**
- **keep rivers clean**
- **cities should protect**
- **small actions can help**
