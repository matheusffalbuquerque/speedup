Root Lesson 10

City Care Journey

In each round, one player becomes the mayor. The other players are citizens and complain about real city problems. The mayor listens, chooses proposals, and presents solutions in English using should, need to, and environmental language.

The teacher controls the screen, starts the timer, rolls the next mayor, and gives a score from 1 to 10 at the end of each 5-minute round.

  • Players: 2 or 3 students
  • Round goal: listen to the citizens and present a city plan
  • Language focus: environment, nature, pollution, recycling, clean public spaces
