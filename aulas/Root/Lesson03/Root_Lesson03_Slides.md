## 🎯 Slide 1 — Welcome & Warm-Up

# Making Plans & Invitations

### 🎮 Warm-Up: "Accept or Decline?"
The teacher says an invitation. Students react:
- 👍 **Thumbs up** = Accept
- 👎 **Thumbs down** = Decline
- 🤷 **Shrug** = Uncertain

*Examples:*
- "Let's go to the gym at 5 AM tomorrow!"
- "Would you like to come to my birthday party?"
- "Why don't we have pizza tonight?"

> **Goal:** Accept, decline, and make plans in English!

## 💬 Slide 3 — Making Invitations

# How to Invite Someone

### ✉️ Formal
> **Would you like to...?**
- *Would you like to come to my party?*
- *Would you like to join us for dinner?*

### 🗣️ Informal
> **Do you want to...?** / **Why don't we...?**
- *Do you want to come over tonight?*
- *Why don't we go out this weekend?*

> **Let's...** / **How about...?**
- *Let's go to the beach!*
- *How about dinner tomorrow?*

## ✅❌ Slide 4 — Responding to Invitations

# Say Yes, No, or Maybe

### ✅ Accepting
| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| I'd love to! | Adoraria! |
| That sounds great! | Parece ótimo! |
| Count me in! | Pode contar comigo! |
| Sure! Of course! | Claro! |

### ❌ Declining (politely!)
| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| I'm sorry, I can't. | Desculpe, não posso. |
| I'd love to, but... | Adoraria, mas... |
| Unfortunately, I can't make it. | Infelizmente não posso. |
| Maybe next time. | Talvez na próxima. |

### 🤔 Not Sure?
- *Let me check my schedule.*
- *Can I get back to you?*
- *I'll let you know.*

## 📅 Slide 5 — Future with "Going to"

# Talking About Plans

### Structure
| | Formula |
|---|---------|
| ✅ | Subject + **am/is/are going to** + verb |
| ❌ | Subject + **am/is/are not going to** + verb |
| ❓ | **Am/Is/Are** + subject + **going to** + verb? |

### Examples
- *I **am going to have** a party next Saturday.*
- *She **is not going to come**.*
- *What **are you going to do** this weekend?*

### Short Answers
- *Are you going to come?* → **Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.**

## 🎭 Slide 6 — Role-Play & Wrap-Up

# Let's Practice!

### 🎭 Dialogue Challenge
Work in pairs — take turns!

**Student A:** Invite B to your birthday party
- Give details: when? where? what time?
- Answer questions

**Student B:**
- Ask questions about the party
- Accept **OR** decline politely
- If accepting: ask *"What should I bring?"*
- If declining: give a reason + say *"Maybe next time!"*

### 📌 Remember
| Invite | Accept | Decline |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Would you like to...? | I'd love to! | I'm sorry, I can't. |
| Do you want to...? | Count me in! | I'd love to, but... |
| Let's...! | That sounds great! | Maybe next time. |

1. Write **3 invitations** (formal, informal, suggestion)
