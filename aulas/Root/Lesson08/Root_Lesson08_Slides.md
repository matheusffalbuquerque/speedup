# Root - Lesson 08: Work & Professions
**Nível:** A1 Avançado / A2 Iniciante  
**Duração:** 60 minutos  
**Tema:** Trabalho e profissões

---

## 🎯 Objetivos da Aula
- Aprender vocabulário central sobre trabalho, carreira e profissões
- Perguntar e responder sobre ocupação com naturalidade
- Usar **I work as...**, **I work in...** e **I work for...**
- Compreender o uso de **Present Perfect** com **for** e **since**
- Falar sobre experiência profissional, rotina e tempo de trabalho

---

## Slide 5: Useful Structures

### Como responder corretamente

| Structure | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **I am a/an + profession** | "I am a teacher." |
| **I work as a/an + profession** | "I work as an engineer." |
| **I work in + place/area** | "I work in an office." |
| **I work for + company** | "I work for a bank." |

### Atenção aos artigos

- ✅ "I am **a** doctor."
- ✅ "She is **an** engineer."
- ❌ "I am teacher."
- ❌ "She is engineer."

---

## Slide 6: Present Perfect with "for" and "since"

### Uso principal

Falamos de uma situação que começou no passado e continua agora.

**Structure:**
```text
Subject + have/has + past participle + for/since
```

**Examples:**
- "I have worked here for three years."
- "She has worked here since 2024."
- "We have known our boss for a long time."

---

## Slide 7: For vs. Since

### Diferença essencial

| Word | Use | Example |
|------|-----|---------|
| **for** | duração | "I have worked here **for** five months." |
| **since** | ponto inicial | "I have worked here **since** June." |

### Mais exemplos

- "He has studied English for two years."
- "She has lived here since 2023."
- "They have had the same job for a long time."
- "I have wanted this career since high school."

---

## Slide 8: Common Mistakes

### Pontos para vigiar

| ❌ Incorrect | ✅ Correct |
|-------------|------------|
| "I am teacher." | "I am a teacher." |
| "She work in an office." | "She works in an office." |
| "I have worked here since three years." | "I have worked here for three years." |
| "He has worked here for January." | "He has worked here since January." |
| "What you do?" | "What do you do?" |
| "I work as engineer." | "I work as an engineer." |

**Model summary:**
- "What do you do?"
- "I work as a receptionist."
- "I have worked here for two years."
