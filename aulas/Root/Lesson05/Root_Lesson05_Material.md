## 📚 Grammar Review

### 1. Comparative Adjectives (Adjetivos Comparativos)

**Quando usar:** Para comparar duas pessoas ou coisas.  
**Significado:** "mais... do que" em português

---

#### RULE 1 — Short Adjectives (1 syllable) → **-ER + THAN**

| Adjective | Comparative | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| tall | tall**er than** | "Pedro is tall**er than** Lucas." |
| short | short**er than** | "Ana is short**er than** her sister." |
| old | old**er than** | "My dad is old**er than** my mom." |
| young | young**er than** | "She is young**er than** me." |
| slim | slim**mer than** | "She is slim**mer than** her cousin." |

**Spelling notes:**
- Adjective ending in **-e** → add only **-r**: nice → nic**er**
- Ending in **consonant + vowel + consonant** → double last letter: big → big**ger**, thin → thin**ner**

---

#### RULE 2 — Long Adjectives (2+ syllables) → **MORE + ADJ + THAN**

| Adjective | Comparative | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| friendly | **more** friendly **than** | "She is **more friendly than** her brother." |
| outgoing | **more** outgoing **than** | "Carlos is **more outgoing than** me." |
| serious | **more** serious **than** | "He is **more serious than** his classmates." |
| generous | **more** generous **than** | "My aunt is **more generous than** my uncle." |

---

#### RULE 3 — Adjectives ending in **-Y** → change **Y → I** + **-ER THAN**

| Adjective | Comparative | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| funny | funn**ier than** | "He is funn**ier than** my brother." |
| lazy | laz**ier than** | "My cat is laz**ier than** my dog." |
| heavy | heav**ier than** | "He is heav**ier than** his twin." |

---

#### RULE 4 — Irregular Comparatives (Irregulares — memorize these!)

| Adjective | Comparative | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **good** | **better than** | "She is a **better** singer **than** me." |
| **bad** | **worse than** | "His day was **worse than** mine." |
| **far** | **farther than** | "The gym is **farther than** the school." |

---

#### ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Erros comuns)

| ❌ Incorrect | ✅ Correct |
|-------------|------------|
| She is **more tall** than me. | She is **taller** than me. |
| He is **more funnier** than her. | He is **funnier** than her. |
| She is **more good** than him. | She is **better** than him. |
| They are **more older** than us. | They are **older** than us. |

### 2. "Looks Like" vs "Is Like" — Important Difference! 🔍

These structures look similar but mean very different things!

---

**"He / She LOOKS LIKE..."** 👁️
- **Use:** Physical appearance — what you can **see**
- **Question:** "What does he/she **look like**?"
- **Meaning:** Como ele/ela parece *fisicamente*

| Example | Translation |
|---------|-------------|
| "She **looks like** a model." | "Ela parece uma modelo." |
| "He **looks like** his father." | "Ele se parece com o pai." |
| "She **looks like** she's 25." | "Ela parece ter 25 anos." |
| "He **looks like** an athlete." | "Ele tem aparência de atleta." |

---

**"He / She IS LIKE..."** ❤️
- **Use:** Personality / character — who they **are**
- **Question:** "What is he/she **like**?"
- **Meaning:** Como ele/ela é *por dentro*

| Example | Translation |
|---------|-------------|
| "She **is like** her mother." | "Ela é parecida com a mãe (personalidade)." |
| "He **is like** a big brother to me." | "Ele é como um irmão mais velho pra mim." |
| "She **is like** sunshine." | "Ela é como um raio de sol (personalidade)." |
| "What **is** she **like**?" | "Como ela é?" |

---

**Side by side:**

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| "What does she **look like**?" | "She is tall with curly brown hair and green eyes." |
| "What is she **like**?" | "She is very friendly, smart, and generous." |

**💡 Quick Memory Trick:**
> **LOOKS LIKE** = 👁️ Eyes → physical (what you *see*)  
> **IS LIKE** = ❤️ Heart → personality (who they *are*)

### Mystery Person — Who Am I?

*Read the descriptions below of three famous people. Answer the questions after each description.*

---

**Description 1:**

*"This person is a woman. She is tall and slim with long, straight blonde hair. She looks like a pop star — elegant and stylish. She is very outgoing and friendly. She loves performing for large crowds. She is more successful than most artists her age, and she is known for being generous with her fans. She is like a storyteller — she turns her feelings into songs."*

**Questions:**
1. What does this person look like? (Hair, height, body type)
2. Is she shy or outgoing?
3. What is she like as a person?
4. What is she compared to in the last sentence?

---

**Description 2:**

*"This person is a man. He is very tall and athletic — taller than most people around him. He has short dark hair and a strong appearance. He looks like a professional athlete. He is known for being smart and serious during competitions, but very funny and outgoing outside of work. He is like a leader — always motivating his team. He is more dedicated than most players in his sport."*

**Questions:**
1. How does this person look? (Height, hair, appearance)
2. Is he serious all the time? Explain.
3. What is he like as a leader?
4. What sport do you think he plays? Why?

*"This person is a woman. She is not very tall, but she looks strong and confident. She has curly dark hair and dark eyes. She looks like a performer — energetic and powerful. She is like a force of nature — unstoppable!"*

**Questions:**
1. What does this person look like?
2. What personality traits are mentioned?
3. What does "a force of nature" mean about her personality?
4. Who do you think this person is?

## 📖 Reading Practice

### Best Friends Forever

My two best friends are Julia and Marcus. They are very different from each other, but I love them both!

Julia is tall and slim, with long curly blonde hair and brown eyes. She looks like a dancer — always graceful and elegant. Her personality is amazing: she is very outgoing and funny. She is friendlier than anyone I know, and she is more generous than most people. Julia is like sunshine — she makes everyone happy when she walks into the room.

Marcus is shorter than Julia, but very athletic. He has straight dark hair and a serious expression on his face. He looks like a scientist or a professor. But don't let his appearance fool you! Marcus is actually very funny and kind once you get to know him. He is shyer than Julia at first, but he is smarter than almost everyone in our class. Marcus is like a quiet storm — calm on the outside, but full of energy inside.

Together, Julia and Marcus are my favorite people in the world. Their personalities are different, but they are both wonderful friends.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. What does Julia look like? Describe her appearance.
2. Is Julia shy or outgoing? Give evidence from the text.
3. How does Marcus look? How is his appearance different from his personality?
4. Who is smarter — Julia or Marcus, according to the text?
5. What do the metaphors mean?  
   a. "Julia is like sunshine."  
   b. "Marcus is like a quiet storm."

## 📌 Quick Reference

### Comparative Rules Summary

| Type | Rule | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| 1 syllable | adj + **-er than** | tall → tall**er than** |
| 2+ syllables | **more** + adj + **than** | friendly → **more** friendly **than** |
| Ends in **-y** | change y→i + **-er than** | funny → funn**ier than** |
| Irregular | memorize! | good → **better** / bad → **worse** |

| Structure | Meaning | Use for |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **looks like** | parece com (visualmente) | Physical appearance 👁️ |
