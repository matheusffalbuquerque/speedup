## 📚 Grammar Review

### 1. There is / There are

Use **there is / there are** to say that something exists in a place.

#### ✅ Affirmative

| Structure | Example | Tradução |
|----------|---------|----------|
| **There is + singular noun** | "There is a sofa in the living room." | Há um sofá na sala. |
| **There are + plural noun** | "There are two chairs in the kitchen." | Há duas cadeiras na cozinha. |
| **There is + uncountable noun** | "There is a lot of furniture in the apartment." | Há muita mobília no apartamento. |

#### ❌ Negative

| Structure | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **There isn't + singular noun** | "There isn't a TV in the bedroom." |
| **There aren't + plural noun** | "There aren't any tables in the room." |

#### ❓ Questions

| Structure | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **Is there + singular noun...?** | "Is there a bed in the room?" |
| **Are there + plural noun...?** | "Are there two windows in the living room?" |

#### Short Answers
- **Yes, there is. / No, there isn't.**
- **Yes, there are. / No, there aren't.**

#### Common mistakes

| ❌ Incorrect | ✅ Correct |
|-------------|------------|
| "There are a bed in my room." | "There is a bed in my room." |
| "There is three chairs in the kitchen." | "There are three chairs in the kitchen." |
| "Have a sofa in the room." | "There is a sofa in the room." |
| "There have many windows." | "There are many windows." |

---

### 2. Prepositions of Place

Use prepositions to show **where** things are.

| Preposition | Português | Example |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| **Between** | Entre | "The table is **between** the sofa and the chair." |
| **Behind** | Atrás de | "The lamp is **behind** the sofa." |
| **In front of** | Na frente de | "The sofa is **in front of** the TV." |
| **Above** | Acima de | "The picture is **above** the bed." |
| **Next to** | Ao lado de | "The bed is **next to** the window." |
| **On** | Em cima de | "The keys are **on** the table." |
| **Under** | Embaixo de | "The shoes are **under** the bed." |
| **In** | Em / dentro de | "There is food **in** the kitchen." |

#### Important contrast: **above** x **on**
- **Above** = acima, sem tocar
- **On** = em cima, com contato

Examples:
- "The picture is **above** the sofa."
- "The phone is **on** the sofa."

#### Important note about **between**
- Usually **between** needs **two reference points**.
- ✅ "The table is between the sofa and the chair."
- ❌ "The table is between the sofa."

### 3. Describing Rooms and Locations

#### Useful patterns
- **There is...**
- **There are...**
- **The [object] is [preposition] the [object].**
- **My favorite room is... because...**
- **I live in a house / apartment.**
- **I want to rent...**

#### Model paragraph

> "I live in an apartment. There is a living room, a bedroom, and a kitchen. In the living room, there is a sofa and a table. The table is in front of the sofa. In the bedroom, there is a bed, and a picture is above the bed."

## 📖 Reading Practice

### My New Apartment

My name is Camila, and I live in a small apartment with my brother. The apartment is not big, but it is comfortable. There is a bright living room, a small kitchen, and two bedrooms.

In the living room, there is a gray sofa and a wooden table. The table is in front of the sofa, and there is a picture above the sofa. There are two plants behind the sofa, near the window.

My bedroom is simple. There is a bed, a lamp, and a closet. The lamp is next to the bed, and my books are on a shelf above the desk. The kitchen has basic furniture, but it is very organized.

We rent this apartment because it is close to work and school. It is simple, but it feels like home.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. Who does Camila live with?
2. Is the apartment big or small?
3. What is in the living room?
4. Where is the table?
5. Where are the plants?
6. Why do they rent this apartment?

## ⚡ Quick Reference

### There is / There are
| Use | Structure | Example |
|-----|-----------|---------|
| Singular | **There is + singular noun** | There is a bed in the room. |
| Plural | **There are + plural noun** | There are two chairs in the kitchen. |
| Negative singular | **There isn't...** | There isn't a TV here. |
| Negative plural | **There aren't...** | There aren't any plants in the room. |
| Question singular | **Is there...?** | Is there a table? |
| Question plural | **Are there...?** | Are there two bedrooms? |

### Prepositions of Place
- **between** = entre

- **in front of** = na frente de
- **above** = acima de
- **next to** = ao lado de
- **on** = em cima de
- **under** = embaixo de
