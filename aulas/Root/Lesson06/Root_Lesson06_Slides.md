# Root - Lesson 06: Home & Furniture
**Nível:** A1 Avançado / A2 Iniciante  
**Duração:** 60 minutos  
**Tema:** Casa e móveis

---

## 🎯 Objetivos da Aula
- Aprender e revisar vocabulário sobre cômodos da casa e móveis
- Usar **there is / there are** para descrever ambientes com mais segurança
- Compreender e aplicar preposições de lugar mais detalhadas: **between, behind, in front of, above**
- Descrever rooms, furniture, and object locations in a natural way
- Falar sobre apartment, rent e a casa dos sonhos em contextos reais

---

## 🎲 Warm-up: Dream House Drawing (8-10 min)

**Dinâmica:** Desenhar a casa dos sonhos e descrevê-la em inglês

**Materiais:**
- Papel ou quadro
- Canetas/lápis
- Opcional: imagens rápidas de casas/apartamentos para inspiração

**Instruções:**
1. Cada aluno desenha rapidamente sua **dream house** ou **dream apartment**
2. O desenho deve incluir pelo menos 3 cômodos e 4 móveis
3. Depois, cada aluno apresenta usando frases simples:
   - "There is a big kitchen."
   - "There are two bedrooms."
   - "The sofa is in front of the TV."
   - "The table is between the sofa and the window."
4. Os colegas fazem 2 ou 3 perguntas:
   - "Is there a balcony?"
   - "Where is the bed?"
   - "Is it a house or an apartment?"

**Para turmas de 1-2 alunos:**
- Professor também desenha e participa
- Compare duas casas: "Which apartment is bigger?" / "Which room is more comfortable?"

**Objetivo:** Ativar conhecimento prévio sobre home vocabulary e preparar os alunos para descrever espaços reais

> 📌 **Teacher tip:** Não corrija tudo no warm-up. Priorize fluência e participação. Anote 2 ou 3 erros recorrentes para retomar mais tarde.

## 📚 Presentation (20 min)

---

### Slide 1: Home Vocabulary — Rooms in the House 🏠

**Vocabulário principal do cronograma:**

| English | Português | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Living room** | Sala de estar | "The family watches TV in the **living room**." |
| **Bedroom** | Quarto | "My **bedroom** is small but comfortable." |
| **Kitchen** | Cozinha | "There is a table in the **kitchen**." |
| **Apartment** | Apartamento | "She lives in a small **apartment** downtown." |
| **Rent** | Alugar / aluguel | "They **rent** an apartment near work." |

**Useful extra room vocabulary:**
- **Bathroom** = banheiro
- **Dining room** = sala de jantar
- **Balcony** = varanda
- **Hallway** = corredor

**Model sentences:**
- "I live in an **apartment**."
- "My **living room** is bright and cozy."
- "We eat breakfast in the **kitchen**."
- "The **bedroom** is next to the bathroom."

> 📌 **Nota do professor:** Reforce a diferença entre **house** e **apartment**. Muitos alunos já conhecem "house", mas precisam usar "apartment" e "rent" em contextos mais reais da vida urbana.

---

### Slide 2: Furniture Vocabulary — What Is in the Room? 🛋️

**Vocabulário principal do cronograma:**

| English | Português | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Sofa** | Sofá | "The **sofa** is very comfortable." |
| **Table** | Mesa | "The books are on the **table**." |
| **Bed** | Cama | "There is a big **bed** in the bedroom." |
| **Furniture** | Móveis / mobília | "This apartment has modern **furniture**." |

**Useful extra furniture vocabulary:**
- **Chair** = cadeira
- **Lamp** = luminária / abajur
- **Closet / wardrobe** = guarda-roupa
- **Shelf** = prateleira
- **TV stand** = rack / suporte da TV

**Natural examples:**
- "There is a blue **sofa** in the living room."
- "There are four chairs around the **table**."
- "The **bed** is next to the window."
- "The **furniture** in this apartment is simple but beautiful."

**Mini focus:**
- **Furniture** is usually **uncountable** in English.
- We say: ✅ "There is a lot of furniture."
- Not: ❌ "There are many furnitures."

---

### Slide 3: There is / There are — Review and Expansion 🧩

**Use:** Para dizer que algo existe ou está em algum lugar

#### ✅ Singular
> **There is + singular noun**

- "There is a sofa in the living room."
- "There is a table in the kitchen."
- "There is an apartment for rent on this street."

#### ✅ Plural
> **There are + plural noun**

- "There are two bedrooms in the apartment."
- "There are four chairs in the kitchen."
- "There are many buildings near my home."

#### ❌ Negative
- "There isn't a TV in the bedroom."
- "There aren't any chairs in the living room."

#### ❓ Questions
- "Is there a sofa in the living room?"
- "Are there two tables in the kitchen?"
- "Is there a bed in your bedroom?"
- "Are there any plants in the apartment?"

#### Short answers
- "Yes, there is." / "No, there isn't."
- "Yes, there are." / "No, there aren't."

**Expansion:**
- **There is some furniture in the apartment.**
- **There are a lot of windows in this house.**
- **There isn't much space in my bedroom.**

**Prováveis erros comuns:**
| ❌ Incorrect | ✅ Correct |
|-------------|------------|
| "There have a sofa in the room." | "There is a sofa in the room." |
| "There are a bed in the bedroom." | "There is a bed in the bedroom." |
| "There is two chairs." | "There are two chairs." |
| "Have a table in the kitchen." | "There is a table in the kitchen." |

> 📌 **Nota do professor:** Muitos alunos brasileiros misturam **have** com **there is/are**. Mostre a diferença: **I have a sofa** = posse; **There is a sofa in the room** = existência/localização.

---

### Slide 4: Prepositions of Place — Advanced Focus 📍

**Target prepositions from the chronogram:**

| Preposition | Português | Example |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| **Between** | Entre | "The table is **between** the sofa and the chair." |
| **Behind** | Atrás de | "The lamp is **behind** the sofa." |
| **In front of** | Na frente de | "The sofa is **in front of** the TV." |
| **Above** | Acima de / em cima de (sem tocar) | "The picture is **above** the bed." |

**Useful review prepositions:**
- **On** = sobre / em cima de
- **Under** = embaixo de
- **Next to** = ao lado de
- **Near** = perto de
- **In** = dentro de

**Visual examples:**
- "The bed is **next to** the window."
- "The table is **between** two chairs."
- "The plant is **in front of** the apartment door."
- "The painting is **above** the sofa."
- "The bike is **behind** the building."

**Important difference:**
- **Above** = acima, sem contato
- **On** = em cima, com contato

Example:
- "The lamp is **on** the table."
- "The picture is **above** the table."

---

### Slide 5: Describing Rooms and Locations Naturally ✨

**Model description:**

> "I live in a small **apartment**. There are two **bedrooms**, one **living room**, and one **kitchen**. In the living room, there is a comfortable **sofa** and a small **table**. The table is **in front of** the sofa, and a lamp is **behind** it. In my bedroom, there is a **bed** and a closet. A picture is **above** the bed. The apartment has simple **furniture**, but I like it a lot."

**Useful sentence frames:**
1. "I live in a house / apartment..."
2. "There is... / There are..."
3. "The [object] is between / behind / in front of / above..."
4. "My favorite room is... because..."
5. "I rent / want to rent..."

**Real-life contexts:**
- describing your home
- explaining a photo of a room
- talking to a friend about a new apartment
- reading an apartment ad

> 📌 **Nota do professor:** Incentive descriptions completas, não apenas listas de palavras. Em vez de "bed, table, sofa", peça frases: "There is a bed in the bedroom." / "The table is between the chairs."

## 💬 Practice Activities (20 min)

---

### Activity 1: Listening Spot the Room (5 min) 🎧

**Type:** Listening

**Teacher reads:**

> "This room has a bed, a lamp, and a closet. There is a picture above the bed. The lamp is next to the bed. There aren't any chairs in this room."

**Students answer:**
1. Which room is it?
2. What furniture is in the room?
3. Where is the picture?

**Second listening:**

> "In this room, there is a sofa and a table. The table is in front of the sofa, and the TV is above the table on the wall. There are two plants behind the sofa."

**Students answer:**
1. Which room is it?
2. Where is the table?
3. Where are the plants?

---

### Activity 2: Speaking — Describe My Room (7 min) 🗣️

**Instructions:**
1. Cada aluno escolhe um room: living room, bedroom ou kitchen
2. Descreve o ambiente sem mostrar desenho/foto
3. Os colegas desenham ou imaginam com base na descrição
4. Depois comparam: "Is my drawing correct?"

**Required language:**
- Use at least **2 there is/are sentences**
- Use at least **2 prepositions of place**

**Example starter:**
- "This is my living room. There is a gray sofa. There is a table in front of the sofa. There are two pictures above the sofa..."

---

### Activity 3: Reading — Apartment for Rent (4 min) 📖

**Mini text:**

> "Apartment for rent: cozy one-bedroom apartment near the city center. There is one bedroom, one bathroom, a small kitchen, and a bright living room. The furniture is simple but modern. In the living room, there is a sofa and a table. In the bedroom, there is a bed and a closet. The apartment is perfect for one person or a couple."

**Quick questions:**
1. Is it a house or an apartment?
2. How many bedrooms are there?
3. What furniture is in the living room?
4. Who is this apartment good for?

---

### Activity 4: Writing — My Ideal Home (4 min) ✍️

**Prompt:**
> Write **4-6 sentences** about your ideal home.

**Students should include:**
- house or apartment
- at least 2 rooms
- at least 3 vocabulary words from the lesson
- at least 2 prepositions of place
- at least 1 sentence with **there is** and 1 with **there are**

**Model answer:**
> "My ideal home is a modern apartment. There is a big living room and there are two bedrooms. In the living room, the sofa is in front of the TV. A picture is above the sofa. In the kitchen, the table is between two chairs."

---

## 📝 Wrap-up (5 min)

**Quick review questions:**
- "What is the difference between **there is** and **there are**?"
- "How do you say **entre** in English?" → **between**
- "How do you say **na frente de**?" → **in front of**
- "Complete: There ___ a bed in the bedroom." → **is**
- "Complete: There ___ two chairs in the kitchen." → **are**
- "Where is the picture if it is over the bed, on the wall?" → **above the bed**

**Final speaking challenge:**
Each student says 3 sentences about their home or dream home:
1. One sentence with **there is / are**
2. One sentence with a furniture word
3. One sentence with a preposition of place

**Homework:**
- Draw your real bedroom or living room and write 6 sentences describing it in English
- Memorize the lesson vocabulary
- Write a short paragraph about an apartment you would like to rent one day

## 📌 Teacher's Notes

### Preparação
- Leve papel para o warm-up ou prepare slides com room layouts simples
- Se possível, tenha imagens de apartment ads ou photos of rooms para speaking prompts
- Escreva no quadro duas colunas: **There is** / **There are**

### Erros comuns a corrigir

| ❌ Erro comum | ✅ Correto |
|--------------|-----------|
| "There have a bed in my room." | "There is a bed in my room." |
| "There are a sofa in the living room." | "There is a sofa in the living room." |
| "The picture is on the bed" (quando está na parede) | "The picture is above the bed." |
| "The table is front of the sofa." | "The table is in front of the sofa." |
| "The chair is between the sofa." | "The chair is between the sofa and the table." |
| "There are many furnitures." | "There is a lot of furniture." |

### Dicas pedagógicas
- Faça gestos com o corpo para ensinar prepositions: fique **behind** a chair, **in front of** the board, etc.
- Desenhos simples ajudam muito mais do que explicações abstratas
- Reforce que **between** normalmente precisa de dois pontos de referência
- Use o vocabulário em contexto real: rent, apartment, room, furniture, location

### Adaptações para turmas pequenas
- **1 aluno:** transforme a aula em descrição guiada com desenho + role-play de apartment visit
- **2-3 alunos:** um descreve, outro desenha, outro corrige
- **4-5 alunos:** faça mini competition: quem descreve o room com mais detalhes corretos?

### Extensão opcional
- Introduzir **this apartment is bigger/smaller than...** para reciclar comparatives da Lesson 05
- Comparar casa real vs dream house
- Criar um mini role-play: landlord and renter
