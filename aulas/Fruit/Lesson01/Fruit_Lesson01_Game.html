📖⚔️ Grammar Story Duel — How to Play

🎯 Objective

Build a collaborative story across rounds while using advanced grammar structures. Each round, two players duel to continue the narrative — the class votes on who used the grammar most naturally and creatively.

🃏 The Decks

  • 📖 Story Card — Sets the narrative context. Story Seeds start a new arc; Chapter Cards continue it.
  • ⚡ Structure Card — The grammar structure the current player MUST use (Mixed Conditional, Inversion, or Cleft Sentence).

🔄 Each Round

  • 1 Draw a Story Card (shared — visible to both players).
  • 2 Draw a Structure Card for Player A.
  • 3 Player A speaks for 75 seconds, continuing the story using their structure.
  • 4 Click "Next Turn" — a new Structure Card is drawn automatically for Player B.
  • 5 Player B speaks for 75 seconds, continuing OR challenging A's narrative.
  • 6 The class discusses: Who used the structure correctly? Who was more coherent and creative? Award points!

🎲 Plot Twist

Draw a twist at any time to add a challenge — shorter timer, vocab requirements, register constraints, or special rules.

🏆 Scoring Suggestions

  • +1 point — Structure used correctly
  • +1 point — Narrative is coherent and creative
  • +1 point — Used vocabulary from the lesson (syntax, nuance, register, etc.)
  • +1 bonus — Class votes as "best continuation of the round"
